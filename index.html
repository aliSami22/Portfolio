Who I Am

I'm a motivated Full Stack Developer based in Damietta, Egypt, with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence from Damietta University and a graduate of the ITI Intensive .NET Full Stack Program.

On the backend, I work with ASP.NET Core, MVC, Entity Framework Core, RESTful APIs, ASP.NET Core Identity, and SQL Server — building reliable, secure, and well-structured server-side systems.

On the frontend, I craft responsive interfaces with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Angular, and React.js — turning design specs into smooth, intuitive user experiences.

I also bring a UI/UX design dimension to my work through Figma, having designed the complete interface for my graduation project, Pharma Hub — a medical mobile app for drug alternatives and pharmacy discovery.
